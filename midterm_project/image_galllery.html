Below are some pictures of alpenglows:

This phenomenon can come in various colors and is not bound to having an effect in one specific way



One of the common colors an alpenglow in is orange which is usually most visible during early morning and evening hours


The second most common color is a red alpenglow, typically occuring during sunset or sunrise


Sometimes there are also pink alpenglows which are most common during late fall and winter months


Purple alpenglows are very rare as they are, but when they are visible, they are more common during sunset than sunrise. This is my favorite type of alpenglow!


The effect of this phenomenon is not limited to the sky and can be seen reflected in the water as well.


One of the most popular places to see an alpenglow is in Half Dome, Yosemite National Park. I hope to come here one day to witness this amazing phenomenon. Maybe we can even have an EBC go here someday!!
